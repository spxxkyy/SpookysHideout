Welcome to Everdark


Everdark is my upcoming passion project:
a point-and-click horror adventure game with a universe that was inspired by the famous Curse of Strahd D&D campaign. The game was built and designed to capture the old-school feeling of those early browser-based point-and-click horror games, and hopefully inspire people to create their own games and revive this nieche style of gameplay!

Take a lantern and explore the secrets that lie within. Fight your way through strange enemies, solve puzzles, and complete quests as you slowly uncover the mysteries of a cursed land ruled by darkness.

Game Features

These are the main features and mechanics that make the game what it is:

Browser based game

No downloads, no unnecessary fluff, native HTML, CSS and javascript. Visit the site and dive right in!

Point and Click Gameplay

A point-and-click gameplay loop combined with the browser based system designed to capture old flash game horror vibes and to revive this style of gameplay.

Streamlined gameplay

No cursor wiggling to find hidden items, no confusing tasks. No fetch quests. Everdark was designed to feel modern and intuitive while keeping the classic feel

Delay based action combat

A unique delay-based combat system inspired by Nodiatis, where timing and weapon choice matters. No mindless clicking, and no boring turn based stuff, this system is a sweet spot in the middle.

In-depth Systems

Every feature is implemented to be deep and engaging while remaining easy to understand at a first glance, whether its combat, magic, skills, or more.

Heavy atmosphere focus

This world relies on immersive visuals and sound design, every location was made with immersion and atmosphere in mind first.

Special enemies and minigames

Special and unkillable enemies with fleshed out personalities, lore and apparences each offering unique minigames, puzzles and challenges for the player, learn their gimmicks and try to avoid them!

Immersive story and progression

Everdark has an interesting and chaotic storyline. Gossip, lies, unnatural entities and world-threatening events. Find out whether you're capable of saving this realm!

Everdark is currently in active development and there is no final release date yet. The goal is to release it once the core gameplay, progression systems, and overall polish hit the quality level the project deserves.

The estimated release date is Halloween 2027.
